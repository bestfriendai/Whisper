Open Firebase Console ‚Üí
  1. Click the "Get Started" button
  2. This will initialize the Authentication service
  3. Wait for the page to load (about 5-10 seconds)
2

Enable Anonymous Authentication

Enable anonymous sign-in for guest users:

  1. In the Firebase Console, click on "Sign-in method" tab
  2. Find "Anonymous" in the list
  3. Click on it and toggle "Enable"
  4. Click "Save"
3

Enable Email/Password Authentication

Enable email/password sign-in for registered users:

  1. Still in the "Sign-in method" tab
  2. Find "Email/Password" in the list
  3. Click on it and toggle "Enable" (first option only)
  4. Click "Save"
4

Optional: Enable Google Sign-In

For easier sign-in, you can also enable Google authentication:

  1. Find "Google" in the sign-in providers list
  2. Click on it and toggle "Enable"
  3. Add a Project support email (your email)
  4. Click "Save"
